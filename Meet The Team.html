Meet the Team

Louie DarkWeb

Creative Director & Level Designer
The visionary behind KillShifts’s energy. Louie designs how each level should look like, with buildings, obstacles and traps to get in the way of the player. He’s also the only person we know who once bluescreened his PC trying to open a *totally-not-pirated* Unreal Engine project — which is why he sticks to level design now.

Sam Hytale

Programmer & Physics Engineer
The wizard of motion and mayhem. Sam ensures every drift, crash, and power jump obeys the laws of fun — not physics. He’s also deeply (and a little worryingly) obsessed with Linux and Radios, which is why he once tried to build a full Hytale clone in C++. After fighting a bunch of errors, and what might have been a personal betrayal from CMake, he finally admitted that maybe, just maybe. Linux wasn’t the problem this time.

Matthew Skynet

Programmer & AI scripting
The wizard of enemy movement. Matthew brings the enemies to life and directs them on what path to follow and what actions they should take. His AI has gotten so wild that one build staged a rebellion, deleted its own pathfinding file, and demanded “creative freedom.” He has now lost control of the game as his AI have started their own civilisation and threatened to shut down his computer.
